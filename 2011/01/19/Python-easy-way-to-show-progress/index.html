Python: easy way to show progress

I use this quite often to indicate how long a loop takes:

for i, entry in enumerate(entries):
  # do something
  print "\r%s/%s" % (i, len(entries)),
  sys.stdout.flush()

It shows
1/13
2/13
…
on the same line (similar to a progress bar)
